  <h3 style="font-family:'Georgia',serif; font-size:2em; margin-bottom:10px;">
    Genomics of high-risk blood and lymphoid cancer
  </h3>
  <ul style="list-style:none; padding:0; margin:20px 0;">
    <li style="font-size:1.1em; margin-bottom:16px; line-height:1.6;">We aim to fully understand blood and lymphoid cancers that resist conventional treatments or relapse after treatment.</li>
    <li style="font-size:1.1em; margin-bottom:16px; line-height:1.6;">We work by combining multi-omics techniques, often simultaneously in individual cancer cells and repeating measurements serially across treatment timepoints.</li>
    <li style="font-size:1.1em; margin-bottom:16px; line-height:1.6;">Our goal is to achieve precise identification of high-risk features earlier in the disease course, and to identify new approaches for targeting the cells and pathways that drive relapse.</li>
